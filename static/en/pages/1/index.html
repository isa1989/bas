ABOUT US

Our wish was to establish a company engaged in scientific research and production that would improve the lives of our people. We created ITL. Our main task was to computerize, set up, provide software, and present it to the customer as a useful tool. We did this with skill. It was 6 and sometimes 8 hours to print information in the state banks at that time. This kept people up to 11-12 in the evening and had to finish printing. We have built the IBM-360 large Electronic Computing Machines with a very fast printing device, our dedicated adapter, and connected to the computer and have created this software-capable (DRIVER). We have commissioned this fast printing system to these banks. The users' joy was beyond their limit, since they now spent a maximum of half an hour to print the data. These banks were the current Kapital Bank and its branches. At the same time, we were able to perform software maintenance on computers and printers to create and maintain software. Our customers were very pleased with us. Since 1997, we have started the sale, installation and maintenance of UPS installations. We have entrusted the service to the new ITS company. We have rapidly mastered the repair of UPS facilities of Turkey's INFORM and French SOCOMEC companies and have begun to successfully perform these services. In order to expand our technical capacities and provide our customers with more advanced technology, we have deployed MAKELSAN, Socomec, and created a new ZUVAND company. Together with UPS we will provide our customers with accurate measuring devices from Fluke, IP cameras of AXIS and many other outstanding techniques. Our goal is to build a smart home, office, produce our own product and create a new technology. God help us,..
